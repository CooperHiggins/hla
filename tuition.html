Screenings are required by the state and are given to all students by Child Health Consultants in October of each year. Parents will be given the results of the tests in a report by the practitioner who conducts the tests. Tuition Installment Program

For the 2013-2014 school year, tuition payments may be made in up to 10 equal installments with the first payment due by August 1st. Families need to furnish finance office with post-dated checks on file, to be deposited on the 1st of each month, or automatic bill pay may be set up.

Tuition Discounts

Prepaid Tuition: A 10% discount will be given to families who pay the tuition IN FULL and a 5% discount will be given if tuition is paid in 2 installments. Payments due August 1st and December 15th. Referrals: A $200 tuition rebate will be awarded each time a new family is referred AND ENROLLS in Harbor Leadership Academy. In order for the discount to be applied, the new family's enrollment fee and first month's tuition must be paid.

Multiple Student Families: Harbor Leadership Academy recognizes that tuition for multiple student families can be very difficult. We offer a 10% discount for the 2nd child, a 15% discount for the third child, and 50% discount for each additional child. Referrals: A $150 tuition rebate will be awarded each time a new family is referred AND ENROLLS in Harbor Leadership Academy. In order for the discount to be applied, the new family's enrollment fee and first month's tuition must be paid.

Regardless of payment program, this is a binding contract; tuition is due in full and is not refundable.
